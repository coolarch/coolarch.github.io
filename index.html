About

Open source projects often start for different reasons. Typically they are created out of need to push a boundry, to prove a concept, make a solution available to a wider audience or just to scratch a figurative itch.

The CoolArch project contributors are highly skilled architects, software engineers and testers that have day jobs and contribute here in their side time for the reasons above.

We welcome your pull requests.
